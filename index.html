RiiConnect your Wii.

RiiConnect24 brings back WiiConnect24, making your Wii more lively.
How to Connect

Services

Here are the services that RiiConnect24 allows you to use.

Forecast Channel

Get local and worldwide weather on your Wii. You can see the weather around the world on a virtual globe.

News Channel

Get national and worldwide news on your Wii. You can see the news around the world on a virtual globe or in a slideshow.

Everybody Votes Channel

Vote on fun polls and see what the RiiConnect24 community has voted on.

Nintendo Channel

Send demos to a DS system and watch videos such as the Nintendo Week series.

Wii Mail

Send messages or game data to other people connected to RiiConnect24.

Check Mii Out Channel

Share Miis with the community and enter them in contests.

RiiTube

Watch YouTube and other video streaming platforms on your Wii.

RiiTag

Share what you've been playing to your friends with a gamertag which supports modern Nintendo devices.

RiiConnect24 Bot

Share your friend codes and other information with our own Discord bot.

Why use RiiConnect24?

RiiConnect24 is a great addition to your Wii, allowing you to use discontinued Channels that were part of WiiConnect24 again. It makes your Wii lively and lets you participate in a community, as you once could.

Whether this will be your first time using these Channels or want some nostalgia, you will enjoy having RiiConnect24 on your Wii.

If you want to play games online that had their online functionality removed with the discontinuation of Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection, please check out Wiimmfi.

About RiiConnect24

RiiConnect24 lets you use the defunct WiiConnect24 service on your Wii once again, allowing you to use the Forecast, News, Everybody Votes, Check Mii Out and Nintendo Channel, along with sending mail to other Wiis.
